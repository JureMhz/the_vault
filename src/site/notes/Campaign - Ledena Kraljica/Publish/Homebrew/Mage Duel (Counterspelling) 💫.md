## 💫 Homebrew Counterspell Mechanic
This system replaces the standard *Counterspell* spell, so that all spellcasters can try to counter a spell.
The counterspell must be logically opposed (e.g., Fire → Water, Teleport → Restraint).
![IMG_4597.png](/img/user/data/Images/IMG_4597.png)


**Odstranitev** uroka _Counterspell_. Uroki se kontrirajo z **logično nasprotnimi** uroki (npr. _Fireball_ $\rightarrow$ _Create Water_, _Misty Step_ $\rightarrow$ _Hold Person_).

## Idea 1 - Spell Duel (minigame)💡 
Any caster can attempt to disrupt a visible spell by using:
- a reaction,
- an unused spell slot,
- and a narratively opposing spell.
*Examples:
Fireball → cold, water, vacuum, stone barrier
Misty Step → binding, gravity, spatial lock 
charm → clarity/pain/fear
ecromancy → radiant/life
illusion → light/truesight/sound disruption*

DM decides if logic is acceptable.
(DM can also adjudicate advantage/disadvantage or ± to a roll.)
*Tip: let countering player log all rolls and modifiers as they are rolled. It should be in the open and suspenseful.*

If accepted:
**Disruptor rolls d10 with spell attack modifier against defenders spell save DC**.
Each spell level difference is ±2 to a roll.
Cantrips are used with disadvantage.
#### Defending against spell disruption
Original caster can defend against disruption in similar way:
- a reaction,
- an unused spell slot, if using a new spell (+2 to resolve)
- or can boost the same spell with any number spell slot.
**Again, roll d10 with spell attack modifier against counters spell save DC.**
#### Teamwork
Anyone at any time (until spell duel is resolved) can lend help with similar spell, using their reaction and spell slots.
They contribute spell attack modifier and used spell slots (+2 per level). 
Higher spell save DC is used when defending.

#### Physical disruption
Anyone adjacent can make reaction melee attack against anyone in spell duel, causing disadvantage to rolls.
#### Resolving
Degrees of success:

| Result                                     | Resolve                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Original caster can break away at any time | Spell cast as disruption takes effect.                                                                                                      |
| Spell is countered >20                     | Both spells take effect on original caster.                                                                                                 |
| Spell is countered >10                     | Spell cast as disruption takes effect.                                                                                                      |
| Spell countered <10                        | **Spell disrupted** and fizzles out. (Minor cosmetic effects possible)                                                                      |
| Equal                                      | **Spells entangle** and last into another round. Casters are unable to do anything but move half speed and have disadvantage on everything. |
| Spell not countered <10                    | **Spell not countered**, takes full effect.                                                                                                 |
| Spell not countered >10                    | **Partial rebound**. Original spell is boosted with half of the effects of spell used as disruption.                                        |
| Spell not countered >20                    | **Full rebound** Both spells take effect on disruptor.                                                                                      |
