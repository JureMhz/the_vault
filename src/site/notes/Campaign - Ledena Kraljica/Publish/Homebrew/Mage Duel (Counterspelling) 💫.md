## 💫 Homebrew Counterspell Mechanic
This system replaces the standard *Counterspell* spell, so that all spellcasters can try to counter a spell.
The counterspell must be logically opposed (e.g., Fire → Water, Teleport → Restraint).
![IMG_4597.png](/img/user/data/Images/IMG_4597.png)


**Odstranitev** uroka _Counterspell_. Uroki se kontrirajo z **logično nasprotnimi** uroki (npr. _Fireball_ $\rightarrow$ _Create Water_, _Misty Step_ $\rightarrow$ _Hold Person_).

## Idea 1 - Spell Duel (minigame)💡 
Any caster can attempt to disrupt a visible spell by using:
- a reaction,
- an unused spell slot,
- and a narratively opposing spell.
*Examples:
Fireball → cold, water, vacuum, stone barrier
Misty Step → binding, gravity, spatial lock 
charm → clarity/pain/fear
ecromancy → radiant/life
illusion → light/truesight/sound disruption*

DM decides if logic is acceptable.
(DM can also adjudicate advantage/disadvantage or ± to a roll.)
*Tip: let countering player log all rolls and modifiers as they are rolled. It should be in the open and suspenseful.*

If accepted:
**Disruptor rolls d10 with spell attack modifier against defenders spell save DC**.
Each spell level difference is ±2 to a roll.
Cantrips are used with disadvantage.
#### Defending against spell disruption
Original caster can defend against disruption in similar way:
- a reaction,
- an unused spell slot, if using a new spell (+2 to resolve)
- or can boost the same spell with any number spell slot.
**Again, roll d10 with spell attack modifier against counters spell save DC.**
#### Teamwork
Anyone at any time (until spell duel is resolved) can lend help with similar spell, using their reaction and spell slots.
They contribute spell attack modifier and used spell slots (+2 per level). 
Higher spell save DC is used when defending.

#### Physical disruption
Anyone adjacent can make reaction melee attack against anyone in spell duel, causing disadvantage to rolls.
#### Resolving
Degrees of success:

| Result                                     | Resolve                                                                                                                                     |
| ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Original caster can break away at any time | Spell cast as disruption takes effect.                                                                                                      |
| Spell is countered >20                     | Both spells take effect on original caster.                                                                                                 |
| Spell is countered >10                     | Spell cast as disruption takes effect.                                                                                                      |
| Spell countered <10                        | **Spell disrupted** and fizzles out. (Minor cosmetic effects possible)                                                                      |
| Equal                                      | **Spells entangle** and last into another round. Casters are unable to do anything but move half speed and have disadvantage on everything. |
| Spell not countered <10                    | **Spell not countered**, takes full effect.                                                                                                 |
| Spell not countered >10                    | **Partial rebound**. Original spell is boosted with half of the effects of spell used as disruption.                                        |
| Spell not countered >20                    | **Full rebound** Both spells take effect on disruptor.                                                                                      |

## Contested rolls ideja
vzami idejo iz arm wrestling pravil.
![Arm Wrestling.png\|700](/img/user/data/Images/Arm%20Wrestling.png)

If caster stops the spell, nothing happens; if counterer stops, the spell is unleashed at last "counter level".
If at any point the caster comes to ≤0, the spell is fully cast.
Others can distract anywhere during spell duel, but can use only one reaction.

Magic duel looks like this:
1. Caster casts damage or controll spell.
2. Counterspeller: roll 1d4 + spell attack bonus + counter modifiers
3. Caster: roll 1d6 + Spellcasting modifier + counter modifiers
4. Counterspeller: roll 1d8 + Spellcasting modifier + counter modifiers
5. Caster: roll 1d10 + Spellcasting modifier + counter modifiers
6. End of round. anyone can stop casting, or proceed with spell duel to the next round.
7. proceed the same, bit with more sided dice.

| Counter modifiers |     |                                                                                      |
| ----------------- | --- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Power             | ±1  | for each spell level                                                                 |
| Can't trip        | -1  | if countering with cantrip                                                           |
| Smartass          | +2  | if you (currently?) know the spell                                                   |
| Teamwork          | ±1  | for each ally and +1 for each level of added spell (reaction)                        |
| Distraction       | ±1  | Reaction: if anyone is distracted during casting (a way for others to play here too) |
### Degrees of Success 📈
The difference between the two contested rolls (Counterspeller's Result - Attacker's Result) determines the outcome:

| Roll Difference | Degree of Success | Effect on Attacking Spell                                                                                                                  |
| --------------- | ----------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| ≥10             | Total Success     | Spell is completely **negated** (wasted and used). The spell used in countering takes effect on caster OR spell is moved to another target |
| 5−9             | Marginal Success  | Spell is **greatly weakened**: Damage ÷2, or DC−4, or redirect the spell.                                                                  |
| 1−5             | Partial Success   | Spell is **slightly weakened**: −1 damage die (or fixed -5 damage), or DC−2.                                                               |
| ≤0              | Fail              | not countered, magical mishap                                                                                                              |

### Example Pairs & Effects 💡

| Attacking Spell (Effect)                                  | Counterspell (Opposition)                                             | Logical Rationale                                                                   |
| --------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Fireball, Firebolt** (Fire/Heat)                        | **Create Water, Ray of Frost** (Water/Cold)                           | Elemental negation: Water douses fire, cold counteracts heat.                       |
| **Lightning Bolt, Shocking Grasp** (Electricity)          | **Absorb Elements (Lightning), Earthen Grasp** (Absorption/Grounding) | Energy absorption or grounding the electrical current.                              |
| **Cone of Cold, Rime's Gust** (Cold/Ice)                  | **Continuous Flame, Heat Metal** (Heat)                               | Elemental negation: Applying heat to melt or counteract the freezing effect.        |
| **Acid Arrow, Melf's Acid Arrow** (Acid)                  | **Create Water, Purify Food and Drink** (Dilution/Neutralization)     | Diluting the acid or applying a purifying magic.                                    |
| **Misty Step, Dimension Door** (Teleportation/Movement)   | **Hold Person, Wall of Force** (Restraint/Obstruction)                | Physically or magically restraining the target or obstructing the dimensional path. |
| **Sleep, Tasha's Hideous Laughter** (Charm/Mental Effect) | **Calm Emotions, Heroism** (Mental Clarity/Resistance)                | Stabilizing the target's emotional/mental state to resist the effect.               |
| **Silence, Stinking Cloud** (Sensory/Area Denial)         | **Thunderwave, Shatter** (Sound/Vibration)                            | Breaking the silence or clearing the disruptive effect with overwhelming force.     |
| **Invisibility, Greater Invisibility** (Concealment)      | **Faerie Fire, See Invisibility** (Revelation)                        | Magic that directly reveals or highlights concealed subjects.                       |
| **Healing Word, Cure Wounds** (Restoration)               | **Inflict Wounds** (Harm/Reversal)                                    | Directly applying harmful magic to oppose the restorative energy.                   |
| **Haste, Freedom of Movement** (Speed/Enhancement)        | **Slow, Web** (Impediment/Slowing)                                    | Applying a magical effect designed to inhibit or slow movement.                     |
Spells with good countring potential:
- Absorb Elements
- Shield
- Dispel Magic (**Predlog za Popravek:** _Dispel Magic_ mora biti **izrecno omejen** na kontriranje _trajne magije_ in _stalnih učinkov_. Če se uporabi za kontriranje uroka, ki je pravkar "v letu", bi moral DM naložiti velik negativen modifikator k metu.)
- Elemental Evocation Cantrips (_Ray of Frost_, _Firebolt_, _Poison Spray_, _Gust_.)
**Zakaj je OP:** Omogočajo **brezplačno** (Cantrip) in **neprestano** poskušanje kontriranja.
Če lahko z _Ray of Frost_ (0. nivo) kontriraš _Fireball_ (3. nivo) in dosežeš Delni Uspeh (npr. znižanje škode), si praktično brezplačno porabil nasprotnikov urok.
**Predlog za Popravek:** Če se Cantrip uporablja kot protipoteza proti **uroku 2. nivoja ali višje**, Protispeller avtomatično prejme kazen **-2 do -4** k metu 2d6, zaradi inherentne šibkosti Cantripa v primerjavi z močno magijo.
