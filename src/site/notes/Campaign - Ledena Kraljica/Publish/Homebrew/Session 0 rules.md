- **Safety tools**: say “Pause for a minute”, everybody gets out of character, and resolves the issue of discomfort.
- **“I know a guy!” rule**: [link](https://youtu.be/q0eCR7OY95Q?t=1057) Basically, if the party needs someone with specific mundane knowledge or skill, one of them can say "I know a guy…" they tell the name and plausible location, then that person exists. The DM makes up relationships and other stuff. No guarantee that they will be favorably inclined. (DM can use CHA save)
- **"I happened to have that thing" rule**: basically, if the party needs a mundane item, a player can declare possession with an explanation of where and why he/she has that item. DM can use INT save if item is too exotic.
- **Identification:** Characters can use downtime to try to identify and attune to those items. The way of tinkering with the object must align with the magical effect the object has, for identification to succeed. DM can ask for a skill check (arcana, nature, dexterity, history,...)
- Must use one **ration (food) per day** when long resting, or suffer 1 level of Exhaustion and recover only half of everything (rounded down).
- **Ignore alignment**. Is just a suggestion for roleplay. Alignment shifts are normal, and happen all the time. *(in a lot of movies this is the peak of the story, bad guy turns good, or the other way around)*
#### Healing and HP
- **Healing potions**:
    - Used as Bonus Action: normal HP dice roll (_normal potion = 4-10 HP_)  
    - Used as Action: max heal (_10_)  
    - Used on an ally is an Action: normal HP dice roll (_4-10_)  
    - If thrown: normal HP dice roll (roll To Hit; range 20/60; heal radius 5 ft.)
- You can use **Help** action to get Downed PC to 1HP. You can use also a bonus action and roll Medicine to heal the PC to that amount of HP.
#### Combat and damage
- **Initiative**: 1d6 + DEX or WIS modifier. *It's less random and has more sense with many different playstyles.*
- **Critical hit** is normal damage die + max damage. 
- **Opportunity attacks** for moving away from an enemy: first enemy doesn't get it, but all subsequent do. Running between or near enemies trigger normal opportunity attacks, but moving carefully 1/2 movement doesn't trigger it. *All that also applies for monsters.*
- You can cast **more leveled spells per turn** just don’t abuse it (sorcerer quicken spell…) 🙂, for example an offensive spell and Misty Step. *Maybe only one attack spell?*
- **Couterspell**: see [[Campaign - Ledena Kraljica/Publish/Homebrew/Mage Duel (Counterspelling) 💫\|Mage Duel (Counterspelling) 💫]]
- Allow normal “**2 weapon fighting**” rule with **unarmed** strikes, left and right hand/leg. You don't add your ability modifier in that bonus attack.
- **Shield Bash** is possible as improvised attack with as bonus action off-hand attack. 1d4+STRmod bludgeoning damage.
- **Pushing** into a wall: take 1d6 extra damage for every 5ft of push movement stopped by hard surface *(a wall)*.
- **Falling**: 1d6 per 5ft (no maximum). Jumping willingly reduces damage by 2d6 (ie: 10 ft; 3m), or more with super successful athletics check.
#### Class specific
- **Reckless Attack** as a reaction (Barbarian).
#### Inspiration (homebrew upgrade)
Other players can suggest an inspiration for coplayer for doing extra heroic deeds.
Inspiration can get you:
- Advantage on any d20 roll
- Turn hit into a crit
- Disadvantage on enemy d20 roll (example: enemy saving throw)
- One free attack or spellcast
- Free reaction
- What happens if more players use inspiration at once?
