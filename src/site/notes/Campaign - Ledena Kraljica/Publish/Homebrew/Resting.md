### Short rest
⏳ **10min**
❤️‍🩹 **Uses hit dice**
Getting a breather
Non stressful easy walking around
### Long rest
⏳ **Nap or sleep for 6h**
❤️‍🩹 **Uses hit dice to heal, recovers half of expended hit dice at the end of sleep (rounded down)**
If you sleep in a bed, you recover half of max hit dice (rounded up).
### Full rest
⏳ **At least one full day of non straneous downtime activity with easting and sleeping.**
❤️‍🩹 **Full heal, recovers all hit dice**
Roleplay: Could be up to a week or month, depending of severity of stress to body and mind and comfort and quality of resting.
